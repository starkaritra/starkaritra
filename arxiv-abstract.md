**Abstract.** I am an Applied Scientist Intern at Microsoft and an M.Tech CSE
student at IIT Roorkee working at the intersection of large language models,
deep learning, and applied ML research. My current work designs **scalable
evaluation frameworks** that systematically measure the quality, reliability,
and safety of LLMs, and **automated benchmarking pipelines** that surface model
failure modes and behavioral regressions. Adjacent threads study **agentic
systems**, **interpretability**, **model compression**, and **machine learning
for physics** (CERN calorimeter data and quantum computing).

**Keywords —** LLM evaluation · RLHF · agentic systems · interpretability ·
model compression · particle-physics ML · quantum machine learning

### Results
- **RLHF alignment.** Reward modeling + PPO on a 1.3B agent: human-preference
  win-rate **52 → 68%**, multi-tool task success **61 → 79%**.
- **Scientific ML.** Vision Transformers on CERN data: **0.91** ROC-AUC,
  outperforming CNN baselines by 10–12%.
- **Efficiency.** Lottery-Ticket pruning retains **88%** accuracy on VGG/ResNet/FCN.
- **Dissemination.** 2 papers under review (post-quantum cryptography; LLM evals);
  interpretability work targeting a workshop.
